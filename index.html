Barnes Witz

Hook + Brief Description

The Barnes Witz is a hypothetical offshoot of the Barnes Foundation’s educational programming—encompassing an experiential website and a fundraising video, both of which are designed to draw teenagers to explore and be inspired by the Barnes Collection, and to underscore the importance of increased teen programming at museums across the country.
